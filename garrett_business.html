Painting

As mentioned early on the "Art page", there are lots of paintings that I have already done. That doesn't necessarily mean that they aren't for sale. If you would like to purchase a painting that I have done or if you have a certain painting you want done, I can do it! I have practiced the Bob Ross wet-on-wet technique for quite awhile and have good experience at nailing down landscapes. In the past, people have commissioned me at roughly $0.25 / square inch of painting. To see what people have ordered in the past, click here! Prints

Having a 3D printer, I have access to print anything I find. I would like to share my access with you! If you already know what you want to print, send me a .stl file of the print and any specifications you might want. If you have a vision of what you want to print but don't have a design, indicate that in the order form and I can design something for you. That being said, more difficult designs are sometimes out of my reach for producing due to the "free" software I used to design. The best place to find designs online is called Thingiverse. Tech

As seen on the Technology page of the site, there are quite a few featured products that I have tinkered with. If you find yourself wanting a prototype or specified piece of tech that I have built, I can do that! For example, if you wanted to purchase a home automation kit including my IR bracelet, a light reciever, and an AC reciever, I could custom fit it to your specific house set up. If you have any awesome ideas for cool arduino-based technology, I am always looking for fun new projects to start so don't be afraid to make suggestions or comments in the form of an email. If you want to order something related to technology, please feel free to email me!
